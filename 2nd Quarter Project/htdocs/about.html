About

This webpage allows people to pick between food or beverages by clicking on the blocks displayed on the screen. They'll be shown four choices of food/beverages after they've chosen their desired meal type. Then they will be choosing any meal/drink of their choice. After they've decided, they should click the block of the food/beverage they wanted. The recipe of the desired meal/drink is shown afterwards. Finally, they'll be making their desired meal!
