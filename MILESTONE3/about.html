ABOUT

This webpage allows people to choose among three well known recipes worldwide and will be able to cook what recipe they've chosen afterwards.
In, the homepage, it shows two icons representing two different victuals (drinks and food). The user should click the icon of the meal of his/her desire.
After clicking on the icon of the victuals of their desire, they will navigate to the next page which contains three icons of different meals.
Then, they must choose the food or drink they want to cook. Afterwards, it will navigate them to the page that contains the ingredients recipe of the meal they have chosen.

"A recipe has no soul. You, as a cook, must bring the soul to a recipe."
