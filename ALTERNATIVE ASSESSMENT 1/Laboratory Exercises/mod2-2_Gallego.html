Kakegurui

Kakegurui is an anime about a girl, named Yumeko Jabami who entered the a school to defeat the student council of the academy. The Hyakkaou Private Academy is a school where gambling instead of school works determines how good a student is. The school is ruled by Kirari Momobami, the president of the student council academy. Jabami has took down several council members on her first week.
